About Me

I am a dedicated scientist working in the field of hydrology and water resources. My work involves understanding and managing water resources to ensure sustainable and equitable access to water for all. I have a passion for studying the interactions between water, the environment, and society, and I strive to develop innovative solutions to address the challenges of water management in the face of climate change.

  • Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) models for predicting water levels in lakes
  • Climate change impact assessments on hydrological cycles
  • Development of sustainable water management strategies
