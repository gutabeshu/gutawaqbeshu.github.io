About Me

I hold a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering (Hydrosystems) from the University of Houston. My dissertation focused on understanding catchment ecohydrological processes and their interactions across multiple spatiotemporal scales. I also have a Master of Advanced Studies in Sustainable Water Resources from ETH Zurich and an M.Sc. in Water Resources Engineering and Management from Addis Ababa University.

My research interests include catchment ecohydrology, urban ecohydrology, medium to large scale hydrological/water resources modeling, water resource systems, hydrological extremes, dynamic risk management, and the resilience of water infrastructures.
